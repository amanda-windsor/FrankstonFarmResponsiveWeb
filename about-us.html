About Us

Frankston Farm was established in 1985 by owner operators Richard and Lynn Frankston. We are a hobby farm and offer authentic farm tour experiences suitable for all ages, and also offer tailored experiences for school and group excursions.

Our Animals

Our farm has horses, cattle, donkeys, sheep, goats, alpacas, rabbits, guinea pigs, chickens, ducks and pigs.

The Snack Bar

Our snack bar is open daily! Our menu includes burgers, hot dogs, sandwiches, hot chips, coffee, tea, juice, soft drinks, ice-cream, and confectionary. Visitors are also welcome to bring their own food for a picnic lunch. The snack bar also sells gift cards (Valid for 12 months from date of puchase).

COVID-19 Requirements

In line with public health direction, all visitors of Frankston Farm are required to be vaccinated against COVID-19. Visitors 16 years of age or older must provide proof of vaccination or medical exemption via the Check in Queensland app.
