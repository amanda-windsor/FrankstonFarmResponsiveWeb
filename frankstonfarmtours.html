Tours

$55 / per person
Children under the age of two are FREE!

Each tour is conducted by a personal guide and we also offer tailored experiences for school groups.

How to book: Please pre-book your tour by phone or email. Payment may be made on arrival at the farm.

Important Information:
All visitors to Frankston Farm must wear enclosed shoes and are encouraged to bring water and wear a hat.
Children under the age of ten must be accompanied by a paying adult.
Visitors are not permitted to bring pets or any animals to the farm.

Tour Times:

Frankston Farm is closed on Sundays and Public Holidays.
Morning and afternoon sessions run daily from Monday to Saturday.
Session Times:
Morning: 8:00am-10:00am
Afternoon: 2:00pm-4:00pm

School Excursions

We would love to host your next School Excursion! The content of the tours can be tailored to suit your curriculum. The following activities are available and can be adjusted to be suitable for various age groups:

Scavenger hunt
Using orienteering skills to navigate the farm
Learning about how our food is produced
Making feed for the farm animals

Tour activities may include:

Tractor trailer ride
Groom and saddling horses
Grooming and leading donkeys on a walk around the farm
Leading alpacas on a walk around the farm
Helping to move sheep and goats
Feeding and petting rabbits, pigs and guinea pigs
Feeding chickens and ducks and collecting eggs
Collecting vegetables from the garden
Enjoying morning or afternoon tea under a fig tree
